Home Automation

What is home automation?

Home automation is the automatic control of the electronic devices in your home and office. These devices can be reffered to as IoT (Internet of Things) or smart devices that are connected to the Internet which allows them to be controlled remotely. Home automation allows these devices to be turned on, off, deliver a notification or update a setting based on instructions that you provide using technologies like a mobile app or voice assistant.

VALUE OF HOME AUTOMATION

What home automation can bring to property values for home in Abilene Texas Real Estate.

According to Master Appraisal Services Smart home technology can definitely add to market value. Appraisers make what they call “adjustments” when they compare tech-enabled homes with similar but tech-deficient houses. Smart homes command higher prices and may be easier to sell as a result. The key is having smart products installed and functioning before you list your home, because most buyers don’t want a prewired home that still needs products installed.

The demographics that are looking for these types of homes span a wide age set and characteristics. Baby Boomers, Parents, Millenials, and Generation Xers are developing an appreciation of what technology can provide and making adding to the demand of home automation. In addition to added security, home automation provides practicality, user friendliness, accessibility and flexibility for the those in your household.

In addition to extra security and peace of mind your family can enjoy the added value for entertainment provided in-house. In a changing world, our home entertainment system is growing in features and access thanks to streaming services like Netflix, Hulu and more. Smart entertainment systems showcase the family opportunities that can be enjoyed.

BENEFITS

How does home automation provide benefits to the homeowner?

With a professional setup, the homeowner can access many areas of the house with a a touch of a screen or speaking a simple voice command. Some of the benefits home automation can provide are

  • Video cameras to monitor who is in and outside your home
  • Video doorbells to inspect who is at your door before approaching it
  • Smart thermostats can adjust based on time of day or if home is empty (during the work day for example)
  • Smart garage door openers can help ensure no one leaves the garage door open again
  • Smart door locks can provide added peace of mind that all the doors are locked
  • Smart lighting system can be programmed to turn on or off based on occupancy or times of day
  • Increase home value
  • Assistive technology to help persons with disabilities to be more independent
  • Convenience to save energy and money on bills
